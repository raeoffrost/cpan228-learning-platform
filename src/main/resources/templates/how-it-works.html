How It Works

How workplace training moves through the portal

The platform keeps internal training simple by giving teams one place to create, browse, and manage course information.

1

Create Training

Managers, trainers, and instructors create workplace learning courses for staff onboarding, safety, compliance, and role-based development.

2

Browse the Catalog

Employees review the available training catalog and use the course list to find learning that fits their role, department, or current responsibilities.

3

Support Ongoing Learning

Teams use the portal as a central reference point for internal knowledge, process updates, and future workplace development activities.

TrainHub is designed to keep internal training simple. Managers, trainers, and instructors create courses so important workplace knowledge can be shared in one place.

Employees browse the available training and choose the courses that match their role, responsibilities, or development goals. This makes it easier for staff to find the learning they need for onboarding, compliance, and everyday work.

Future versions of the platform may expand to include features such as progress tracking, assessments, and reporting to support a more complete workplace learning experience.
