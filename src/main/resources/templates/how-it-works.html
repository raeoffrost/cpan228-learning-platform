How It Works

Learning Platform is designed to keep employee training simple. Instructors create courses so that important workplace knowledge can be shared with employees in one place.

Learners browse the available training and choose the courses that match their role, responsibilities, or development goals. This makes it easier for employees to stay current with internal learning opportunities.

Future versions of the platform may expand to include features such as assignments, progress tracking, and grading to support a more complete learning experience.
