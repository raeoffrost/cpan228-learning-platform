About Learning Platform

Learning Platform is an internal training website designed to help organizations manage employee learning in a clear and organized way. It provides a central place where workplace training can be shared and reviewed.

The platform can support employee onboarding by introducing new hires to company expectations, tools, and processes. It can also be used for safety training, product knowledge, and other internal learning activities that help teams stay informed and prepared.

The goal is to make workplace learning easier to access while giving instructors a simple way to offer useful training content across the organization.
