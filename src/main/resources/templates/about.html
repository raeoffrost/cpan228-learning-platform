About the Portal

About TrainHub

TrainHub is designed as a simple internal workplace learning center for employee training and development.

TrainHub is an internal employee training website designed to help organizations share workplace learning in a clear and organized way. It gives teams one central place to access training materials that support day-to-day work and long-term development.

The platform can be used for employee onboarding, compliance training, safety topics, product and process learning, and other internal development needs. It helps new and existing employees stay informed about company expectations, tools, and workplace procedures.

The goal is to make workplace training easier to access while giving managers, trainers, and instructors a simple way to provide useful learning content across the organization.

Common Uses

  • New employee onboarding
  • Compliance and policy refreshers
  • Safety and operational training
  • Product and process learning

Internal Focus

The portal is built for workplace learning, not public course sales or academic delivery.
